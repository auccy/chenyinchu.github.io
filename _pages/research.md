## Research

<div class="jumbotron">
<div class="col-md-12 col-sm-12">

<h4>Interpretable Machine Learning for Dementia Risk Prediction</h4>

<p>
I develop interpretable machine learning models for Alzheimer’s disease and mild cognitive impairment (MCI) using large-scale cohort datasets such as NACC, ROSMAP, and AIBL. My work focuses on translating complex models into clinically actionable tools.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Risk prediction and early detection of dementia</li>
<li>Interpretable frameworks (AutoScore, SHAP, LIME)</li>
<li>Multi-cohort validation across international datasets</li>
</ul>

</div>
</div>

<div class="jumbotron">
<div class="col-md-12 col-sm-12">

<h4>Multi-Pathology and Multi-Label Prediction</h4>

<p>
I investigate multi-label prediction of co-existing neuropathologies (e.g., Alzheimer’s disease, vascular pathology, Lewy body disease) using baseline exposome and clinical variables.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Multi-label classification for complex disease outcomes</li>
<li>Integration of clinical, demographic, and biomarker data</li>
<li>Improving precision diagnosis and disease stratification</li>
</ul>

</div>
</div>

<div class="jumbotron">
<div class="col-md-12 col-sm-12">

<h4>Causal Inference and Treatment Effect Modelling</h4>

<p>
I apply causal inference methods to evaluate treatment effects and disease progression, including inverse probability weighting (IPW), overlap weighting, and causal machine learning approaches.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Target trial emulation using observational data</li>
<li>Heterogeneous treatment effect estimation</li>
<li>Drug repurposing in neurodegenerative diseases</li>
</ul>

</div>
</div>

<div class="jumbotron">
<div class="col-md-12 col-sm-12">

<h4>Exposome and Disease Progression Modelling</h4>

<p>
My work explores how lifestyle, environmental, and clinical exposures (the exposome) influence dementia onset and progression through longitudinal modelling frameworks.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Late-life exposome analysis</li>
<li>Integration of lifestyle and clinical risk factors</li>
<li>Longitudinal disease trajectory modelling</li>
</ul>

</div>
</div>
